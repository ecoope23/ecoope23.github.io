Where to Find Us

We are located at the North Carolina Research Campus in Kannapolis, just a few miles north of the city of Charlotte. The NCRC is a unique and collaborative research environment that is home to labs from multiple North Carolina universities and companies.

The Cooper lab can be found on the 3rd floor of the David H. Murdock Research Institute building (pictured below).

UNC Charlotte Main Campus

The department of Bioinformatics and Genomics is located at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte main campus, in the Bioinformatics building. Dr. Cooper's office on campus can be found in room 271.
